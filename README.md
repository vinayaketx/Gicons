**Gicons** is a powerful, lightweight Chrome Extension designed to supercharge your Gmail workflow. It intelligently injects a customizable row of quick-action icons directly onto every email row in your inbox, and into the top toolbar when viewing a message. 

Manage your inbox faster than ever—archive, delete, snooze, mark as read, or run custom filters without ever opening an email.

---

## ✨ Key Features

*   **🖱️ Native Actions:** Archive, Delete, Mark as Read, and Snooze emails with a single click.
*   **🔍 Custom Filters:** Instantly filter your inbox by "Sender" or "Domain" to find related emails.
*   **📋 Quick Copy:** One-click copy of the sender's email address to your clipboard.
*   **🚫 Spam & Unsubscribe:** Mark annoying emails as spam or quickly find and trigger "Unsubscribe" links.
*   **🎨 Premium Settings Page:** A beautiful, glassmorphism-styled dashboard with Light and Dark mode.
*   **⚙️ Highly Customizable:**
    *   **Drag-and-Drop:** Reorder your icons exactly how you want them.
    *   **Toggle Visibility:** Hide actions you don't use.
    *   **Custom Icons:** Upload your own SVG or PNG icons to match your personal style.
    *   **Layout Controls:** Choose between left/right alignment and adjust icon spacing.
*   **⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts:** Set custom keyboard shortcuts (Default: `Ctrl+Shift+F`) to instantly trigger actions on the email you are currently hovering over.

## 🛠️ Installation Guide

Since this extension is not yet on the Chrome Web Store, you can easily install it locally in "Developer Mode".

### Step 1: Download the Code
1. Click the green **"Code"** button at the top right of this repository.
2. Select **"Download ZIP"**.
3. Extract the downloaded ZIP file to a folder on your computer.

### Step 2: Load into Chrome
1. Open Google Chrome.
2. Type `chrome://extensions/` into your address bar and hit **Enter**.
3. In the top right corner, turn on the switch for **"Developer mode"**.
4. In the top left corner, click the **"Load unpacked"** button.
5. Select the folder where you extracted the Gicons files.

🎉 **That's it!** The extension is now installed.

## 💡 How to Use

1. **Open Gmail** (`mail.google.com`) and refresh the page if it's already open.
2. **Hover over any email** in your inbox list. You will see the new Gicons toolbar appear instantly.
3. **Click an icon** to perform the action.
4. **Open an email** to see the Gicons toolbar conveniently placed next to the native "More" (3 dots) menu at the top.

### Customizing your Settings
1. Look for the puzzle piece icon 🧩 in the top right of your Chrome browser (next to your profile picture).
2. Click it, find **Gicons**, and click the **three dots** next to it.
3. Select **"Options"**.
4. Enjoy the beautiful settings page where you can upload custom icons, change colors, and reorder buttons!

## 🏗️ Technical Details

Gicons is built with modern, vanilla web technologies. It requires **zero external dependencies**, ensuring maximum performance and privacy.

*   **Manifest V3:** Follows Google's latest, most secure extension architecture.
*   **Vanilla JavaScript & CSS:** No React, no jQuery. Just fast, optimized DOM manipulation.
*   **Privacy First:** All settings are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. No data ever leaves your browser.
*   **Non-Destructive DOM Injection:** Carefully engineered to integrate with Gmail's complex structure without breaking native functionality or causing layout shifts.
